julia_chess : (early in development) An image capture based chess player named Julia, using the python-chess package for move validation and generation. For more information visit here. From the GitHub page:

Julia is a chess player in the making, and as of now she shies away from playing. Once ready, Julia will make use of a webcam placed directly above a chessboard to analyse and validate your moves (functionality provided by the python-chess package). After thinking a bit, Julia will then (kindly) ask you to play her move on the chessboard. Beware however - Julia will be smart enough to realise if you have cheated when moving her piece.
